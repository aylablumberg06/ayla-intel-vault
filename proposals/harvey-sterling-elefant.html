02 · Where It Breaks

The real bottlenecks.

Every business in your shape hits the same four walls. They're the problems worth solving because solving them moves the number — not the vanity metrics.

Finding the right cross-border partner is slow and expensive

Every market has maybe 30 real targets. Finding them manually — and separating the signal companies from the noise — burns weeks. And then you still have to get a meeting.

Research depth vs. speed is an impossible trade-off

You can do shallow research on 20 companies or deep research on 3. The deal you need is hidden in company #14. Humans can't win this math.

Strategic framing changes per region

The pitch that works in Dubai doesn't land in Singapore. Every deck needs to be written from scratch — which is why most BD shops send the same one to everyone.

Follow-through dies in the gap between first meeting and deal close

The second, third, and fourth touches are where deals actually happen — and they're exactly the touches that slip when you're on a plane.
