02 · Where It Breaks

The real bottlenecks.

Every business in your shape hits the same four walls. They're the problems worth solving because solving them moves the number — not the vanity metrics.

You can't scale client acquisition without hiring a BDR

Every new client comes from your network or a webinar you hosted. Both work. Neither scales. A BDR costs $80K+ before you see the first reply.

Cold email has a reputation problem — yours has to be different

Generic 'we saw your site could use SEO' emails land in spam. Personalized, audit-backed outreach gets replies. The math isn't close. But writing that manually? Impossible.

Prospects want to see work that looks like theirs before they book a call

A dentist wants to see dentist sites. A boutique owner wants boutique. You can't have 200 case studies — but you can personalize the pitch around each prospect's actual niche.

Follow-up drops — hot leads go cold

The third email is where deals get booked. It's also the email that never gets sent when you're heads-down delivering for current clients.
