Strategic Recommendation
Next Steps & Execution Timeline

Board Recommendation: Option B

For Matthias John Realty's market position and growth objectives, we recommend Option B — Full Build as the optimal initial deployment.

This tier delivers the complete 6-agent framework while maintaining fixed costs and predictable budget allocation. The weekly reporting cadence and bi-weekly strategic check-ins ensure continuous optimization and rapid response to market opportunities.

Within 90 days, validated performance metrics will clearly indicate whether Option C's commission structure becomes the natural evolution — transitioning to a true partnership model once baseline results are established.
