Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Ayla Blumberg ("Contractor") and Ninety Five Media, represented by Emma Tessler ("Client").

2
Scope of Work

Contractor will design, build, and deploy an AI-powered lead generation system (the "System") consisting of the following components:

  • Four primary AI agents: Lead Research Agent, Content Preview Agent, Outreach Agent, and Orchestrator Agent
  • Instagram profile scraping via Apify for lead discovery and qualification
  • Canva-generated content previews included in outbound emails
  • Automated Gmail draft placement with voice and tone learning based on Client's style
  • Lead scoring dashboard for pipeline tracking and reporting
  • Deployment to a dedicated cloud server owned and controlled by Client
  • Creation of a dedicated email account by Contractor for all third-party service setups

Scope is limited to the components listed above. Any additional features or work outside this scope require a separate written agreement. Outbound emails will include both Canva-based visuals and initial strategic messaging developed collaboratively with Client.

3
Third-Party Costs

Client is responsible for all third-party service costs required to operate the System, including but not limited to: API usage fees, cloud server hosting, Instagram scraping credits (Apify), and any other platform or token costs. These costs are to be billed directly to Client's payment method and are separate from and in addition to Contractor's fees under this Agreement.

Commission obligations survive termination of this Agreement. Client shall owe the $500 commission for any lead that: (a) appeared in the pipeline dashboard during the active retainer period, and (b) signs a client agreement with Ninety Five Media within 12 months following the termination date.
