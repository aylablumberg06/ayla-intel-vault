What I See

You've Already Built Something Beautiful

Your website, booking flow, partner program, referral system, and resource hub are all in place — that's rare for a pre-launch business. The foundation is strong.

Where AI agents come in isn't about replacing what you've built. It's about making the machine run while you're focused on the ministry — automating the repetitive grind so you can spend your time on the rides, the community, and the vision.

Opportunities

Where AI Agents Could Help

These are initial ideas based on your business model. Everything is flexible — we'd shape the final scope together based on what matters most to you.

01
Outreach

Church & Ministry Outreach Agent

An agent that researches churches, youth groups, and ministries across South Florida and sends personalized outreach on your behalf — introducing The Worship Wawa and offering group booking packages.

Think of it as a tireless team member who finds the right contacts, crafts a message that sounds like you, and follows up automatically if they don't respond.

What it could do
Find churches in your service area Pull contact info & social profiles Send personalized intro emails Auto follow-up sequences Track who's responded
02
Booking Follow-Up

Lead Nurture & Booking Recovery

When someone visits your FareHarbor page but doesn't book, or fills out a contact form but goes quiet — an agent follows up with a warm, personal message to bring them back.

Also handles post-inquiry follow-ups: someone emails asking about a Celebration ride? The agent responds instantly with details and nudges them toward booking.

What it could do
Instant inquiry responses Abandoned booking follow-up Personalized booking nudges FAQ auto-responses
03
Post-Ride

Post-Ride Experience Agent

After every ride, an agent reaches out to guests with a thank-you message, asks for a testimonial or review, and invites them into your referral program. Turns one-time riders into repeat bookers and advocates.

What it could do
Automated thank-you messages Review & testimonial requests Referral program enrollment Re-booking offers
04
Partnerships

Partner Pipeline Manager

You already have a "Partner With Us" page where businesses apply to be route stops. An agent could actively seek out Christian businesses, cafes, and venues along your routes and reach out to them — instead of waiting for applications to come in.

What it could do
Find businesses on your routes Send partnership pitches Track application status Follow up with pending partners
05
Social

Social Media & DM Handler

If you're getting inbound DMs on Instagram or TikTok asking about rides, pricing, or availability — an agent can respond instantly in your voice, answer common questions, and direct people to book.

What it could do
Instant DM responses Pricing & availability info Direct to FareHarbor booking Capture leads from comments
Being Honest

You Might Not Need All of This

You're pre-launch with 3 rides/day max capacity. A full 5-agent system would be overkill right now. The smart move is to start with 1–2 agents that solve your most pressing problem — probably outreach and lead follow-up — and add more as you grow.

I'd rather build you something lean that actually works than sell you a system you'll outgrow the need for before you even use it.
