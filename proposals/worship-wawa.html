The Vision

Automation That Moves With You

The Worship Wawa is launching something special — and the goal here is to make sure you have an intelligent system running behind the scenes from day one. Not a massive enterprise build, but a lean, focused set of tools that handle the repetitive work so you can focus on what matters: the rides, the community, the ministry.

Two tiers below — Essentials gets you running fast with what matters most, and Full Build adds the crew infrastructure as your team grows. You can start with one and upgrade anytime.

Included in Both Tiers

The Outreach Engine

An AI agent that researches churches, youth groups, and faith-based organizations across South Florida and sends personalized outreach in your voice — introducing The Worship Wawa and pitching group bookings, partnerships, and special event rides. Follows up automatically with non-responders.

Post-Ride Follow-Up Agent

After every ride, this agent automatically sends a personalized thank-you message to the booking contact. Includes a review link (Google, FareHarbor), an invitation to join the referral program, and a prompt to rebook. Turns one-time riders into repeat customers and word-of-mouth advocates.

Crew Recruitment & Scheduling

The Full Build adds two more outreach agents for finding drivers and DJs, plus a custom-built crew scheduling dashboard — a web application where your approved team members can see upcoming rides and claim shifts. This is the most technically complex piece of the project because it's a standalone app with user accounts, ride management, and real-time availability.

Crew Scheduling Dashboard

A custom web application where your approved drivers and DJs can log in, view upcoming booked rides, and claim the ones they want to work. You approve claims and lock in the schedule. No more group texts — one source of truth for your entire crew.

This is the most involved deliverable in the project — it requires building a standalone application with user authentication, ride management, and a claim/approval workflow. That's why the Full Build carries a higher price point.
